Rashes When to Contact a Medical Professional

Call 911 or seek emergency help if:
- Shortness of breath
- Tight throat
- Facial swelling
- Purple bruise-like rash in children

Contact your provider if:
- You have joint pain, fever, or sore throat
- Rash has red streaks, swelling, or tenderness (possible infection)
- You are on a new medication (do not stop without consulting your provider)
- Suspected tick bite
