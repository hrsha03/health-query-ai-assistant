Rashes What to Expect at Your Office Visit

Your provider will examine your skin and ask about:

- Onset of rash
- Location and spread
- Factors that improve or worsen the rash
- New products or exposures
- Insect or tick bites
- Changes in medications or diet
- Itchiness or scaling
- Existing allergies/asthma
- Recent travel

Tests may include:
- Allergy tests
- Blood tests
- Skin biopsy or scraping

Treatment may involve:
- Prescription creams/lotions
- Oral medications
- Minor skin surgery (if needed)

You may be referred to a dermatologist for complex cases.
