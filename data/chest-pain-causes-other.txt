Other causes of chest pain:

- Panic attack, often with fast breathing
- Costochondritis: Inflammation where the ribs join the breast bone
- Shingles: Sharp, tingling pain on one side of the chest or back, often with a rash
- Muscle strain: Involving muscles or tendons between the ribs
- Digestive system issues: GERD, gallstones, esophageal spasms, stomach ulcer, or gastritis
