Dizziness 
What to Expect at Your Office Visit

Your provider will perform a physical exam and ask questions about your medical history and symptoms, including:
- When did your dizziness begin?
- Does your dizziness occur or worsen with movement?
- What other symptoms occur with the dizziness?
- Is the dizziness constant or does it come and go?
- How long does an episode last?
- Was there a recent cold, flu, or other illness prior to the symptoms?
- Are you experiencing stress or anxiety?

Tests that may be performed include:
- Blood pressure measurement
- Electrocardiogram (ECG)
- Hearing tests and balance testing (ENG)
- Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
