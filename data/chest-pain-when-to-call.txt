Chest Pain 
Call 911 or your local emergency number if you have:

- Sudden crushing, squeezing, or pressure in the chest
- Pain spreading to the jaw, left arm, or between the shoulder blades
- Associated nausea, dizziness, sweating, or shortness of breath
- Known angina that suddenly worsens, or symptoms occurring while at rest
- Sharp chest pain with shortness of breath, especially after immobility or surgery
