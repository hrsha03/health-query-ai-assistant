Rashes Home Care

Most mild rashes improve with gentle care and avoiding irritants. Recommendations:

- Avoid scrubbing the skin
- Use gentle cleansers
- Donâ€™t apply lotions/creams to the rash unless advised
- Wash with warm (not hot) water and pat dry
- Stop using recently introduced skincare/cosmetic products
- Expose the rash to air as much as possible
- Use calamine lotion for poison ivy/oak/sumac or contact dermatitis
- Apply 1% hydrocortisone cream (OTC) to soothe irritation
- For eczema: Moisturize frequently, consider oatmeal baths
- Oral antihistamines may relieve itching
