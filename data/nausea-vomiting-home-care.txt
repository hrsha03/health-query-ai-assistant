Nausea Vomiting Home Care

After identifying the cause, treatment may involve:
- Taking medication
- Changing your diet or habits
- Drinking small amounts of clear liquids frequently

For pregnancy-related morning sickness, consult your provider.

For motion sickness:
- Stay still
- Use over-the-counter antihistamines (e.g., dimenhydrinate/Dramamine)
- Consider prescription scopolamine patches (adults only; follow provider instructions)

Do not give scopolamine to children.
