Panic disorder When to Contact a Medical Professional

Contact your provider if panic attacks are affecting your life, work, or self-esteem.

**If you or someone you know is thinking about suicide:**
- Call or text 988
- Chat at 988lifeline.org
- Or call 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255)

**In emergencies:**
- Call 911
- Go to the nearest ER
- Do NOT leave someone alone if they are suicidal
