Nausea Vomiting Causes

Common causes of nausea and vomiting include:
- Food allergies
- Infections of the stomach or bowels (e.g., stomach flu, food poisoning)
- Gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD)
- Medicines or medical treatments (e.g., chemotherapy, radiation)
- Migraine headaches
- Morning sickness during pregnancy
- Motion sickness or seasickness
- Severe pain (e.g., kidney stones)
- Excessive marijuana use

Possible serious causes:
- Appendicitis
- Intestinal blockage
- Cancer or tumors
- Ingesting drugs or poisons (especially in children)
- Stomach or small intestine ulcers
